Reflections from Kosova

A Photographic Journey Through the Land That Inspired the Novel

Over the course of writing A Murder of Crows Fly with a Red Ribbon, Carol Detweiler made numerous trips to Kosova — walking the villages, the mountains, and the memorials that shape Ariana's story. These photographs are windows into the landscape of the novel: the Balkan peaks that watched over the war, the quiet streets where families were torn apart, and the resilient beauty of a country still writing its future.
